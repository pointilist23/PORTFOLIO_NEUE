Fear of God.

Logo and Brand design.

At the end of my first year at Bath College, I was tasked with a Final Major Project which is a project which I was left alone to ideate, execute, and submit to be graded. I chose the brand Fear of God to rebrand since the brand was rather flexible in how it was represented, and as a base had rather minimalistic branding. I also was inspired by some work by Joe Perez. I wanted to make the brand a more high-speed high-energy visual whilst still aligning with the brand values and ethos.
